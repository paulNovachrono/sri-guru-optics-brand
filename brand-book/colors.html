Accessibility

All primary combinations meet WCAG 2.1 AA requirements for body text.

Passing Combinations

#1A365Don whiteAA ✅
whiteon #1A365DAA ✅
#0EA5E9on whiteAA ✅
#D4AF37on #1A365DAA ✅

Guidelines

  • •Never place Gold (#D4AF37) on white — fails contrast
  • •Use Deep Navy or dark backgrounds for Gold text
  • •Body text must be Deep Navy on white or white on Deep Navy
  • •Teal (#0EA5E9) is accent-only — not for body text
  • •Maintain minimum 4.5:1 contrast for body copy
