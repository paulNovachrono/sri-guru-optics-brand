Brand Pattern

Subtle Spectacle Pattern

A repeating micro-pattern of the spectacles icon, used as a watermark on packaging, website backgrounds, and stationery. Always rendered at 20% opacity over Deep Navy or as a subtle tone-on-tone effect on light backgrounds.

Usage: Gift boxes, tissue paper, website hero backgrounds, envelope liners.

04

Image Guidelines

Do

  • ✓Show real people in genuine interactions
  • ✓Use warm, natural lighting with soft contrast
  • ✓Include context — a person using their glasses, reading, working
  • ✓Frame close-ups of glasses with shallow depth of field
  • ✓Use the brand color palette as a colour grading guide

Don't

  • ✗Use overly staged or clearly stock photography
  • ✗Crop faces awkwardly — respect the person in the frame
  • ✗Apply heavy filters or unrealistic colour grading
  • ✗Show dirty or scratched glasses in product shots
  • ✗Use images that feel cold, clinical, or intimidating
